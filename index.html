Degen Raffle Project

Blockchain

Developed "Degen Raffle", an innovative project on the Solana blockchain that allows users to mint unique NFTs serving as raffle tickets. The project leveraged the Hashlips Art Generator to create visually appealing and unique NFT tickets. The backend was powered by Candy Machine V2, enabling robust and scalable smart contracts to handle raffle entries, ticket draws, and prize distributions securely and transparently.

The project was launched with a strategic marketing campaign targeting niche communities on Crypto Twitter (CT) and Discord, resulting in high engagement and a vibrant community build-up. This approach not only drove initial traffic but also established a sustainable user base, keen on participating in blockchain-based raffles. The campaign effectively demonstrated the practical utility of NFTs beyond art.
